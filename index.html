About JCM
“Precision. Detail. Excellence.”

JCM Barbershop is a licensed, appointment-only barbershop based in Hilo, Hawaiʻi, providing haircuts — including custom designs and fades — plus friendly service, rated 4.9 stars across 159 Google reviews. Every visit is built around one chair, one client, and full attention.

From clean skin fades to intricate hair designs, the standard never moves: precise lines, careful detail work, and a finished cut that holds up days later. Reserve your seat online and walk in to a chair that’s ready for you.

“We are proud to serve the Hilo community! As seen from Keawe Street, our JCM sign is posted outside of door 120 — the barbershop is located up the stairs to the left, door 204. Mahalo!”

Good to Know

Booking
policy.

I.

24-Hour Notice

We respect everyone’s time and keep the schedule running smoothly. Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment.

II.

$25 Late Cancellation Fee

Appointments canceled with less than 24 hours’ notice will incur a $25 late cancellation fee. This helps us accommodate other clients waiting for a seat.

III.

By Appointment Only

JCM Barbershop is licensed and appointment-only — no walk-ins. Reserve your seat online ahead of time to guarantee your spot.
